Delete Your Echo Account

You can request the deletion of your Echo account and all associated data at any time.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All journal entries and voice notes
  • Your profile and preferences
  • AI conversation history
  • Push notification tokens

What may be retained

  • Anonymized usage data for up to 30 days for security and fraud prevention purposes

How to delete your account

Option 1 — In the app:
Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account and confirm the deletion.

Option 2 — By email:
Send a deletion request to support@echo-app.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.

We will process your request within 7 business days.
